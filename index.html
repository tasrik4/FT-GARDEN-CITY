About FT Garden City

FT Garden City is an IoT-based smart plant watering system that helps you maintain your garden effortlessly. Our system monitors soil moisture levels and automatically waters your plants when needed, ensuring optimal growth conditions.

With our mobile-friendly dashboard, you can control and monitor your garden from anywhere, at any time.

How to Use

Follow these simple steps to get started with your smart garden:

Setup

Connect your device to power and place the moisture sensor in your plant's soil.

Control

Use the dashboard to switch between auto and manual modes, or water plants instantly.

Schedule

Set up watering schedules for specific days and times that work best for your plants.

Monitor

Track soil moisture levels and watering history to understand your plants' needs.

System Features

Our smart watering system includes these advanced features:

  • Real-time soil moisture monitoring
  • Automatic and manual watering modes
  • Customizable watering schedules
  • Watering history and system logs
  • Mobile-friendly responsive dashboard
  • Secure cloud-based data storage
  • Device status monitoring
